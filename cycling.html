Cycling Trips
Red Carpet’s cycling trips cater to the travel needs of adventure lovers, hardcore cyclists and mountain bikers. Our cycling trip takes you to villages and trails less frequented, to isolated monasteries and village shrines.

Furthermore, our cycling trips cover a variety of altitude and topographies. We go as low as 60 ~ meters, and as high as 5500 meters above sea-level, providing a really diverse riding experience to cyclists.

Are you a cyclist looking to brave the Himalayan region on two wheels? Browse our cycling trips below!
