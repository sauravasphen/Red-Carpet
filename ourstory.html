Our Story
Red Carpet Journey was founded in 2012 by Mr. K.P. Pathak, a filmmaker with a passion for travel and adventure. After spending nearly two decades traveling the world, Mr. Pathak was inspired to create a tourism enterprise that would provide unique and authentic experiences for travelers and contribute to the local economies and societies of the places he visited.

On a trip to the rural area of Jumla in western Nepal, Mr. Pathak was struck by the kindness and hospitality of the people he met, even in their poverty. He knew that he wanted to do something to help these communities, so he founded Red Carpet Treks & Adventure Pvt Ltd to conduct tours to these rural areas to contribute to their betterment.

Today, Red Carpet Journey is a leading travel brand in Nepal, known for its commitment to sustainability, authenticity, and providing enriching experiences for its travelers. Whether you’re seeking an adventurous trek through the Himalayas or a cultural tour of Nepal’s vibrant cities, Red Carpet Journey has something for everyone. So, trust us for your journey to make it unforgettable.
