Our Services

At Red Carpet Tours & Travels Pvt Ltd, we strive to be the ultimate one-stop shop for all your travel needs. Red Carpet Journey has the expertise and experience to help you realize your travel dreams, from organizing and crafting customized trips to handling meetings, conferences, and exhibitions.

Here are a few of the ways we can assist you in planning the perfect trip:

Trip Organizer

Whether you’re interested in one of our pre-planned trips or want us to create a tailored itinerary, we’re here to make it happen. Browse our trips online or contact us to start planning your dream vacation.

Meeting Incentives Conferences Exhibitions

We offer top-notch services as a mice operator in Nepal for businesses, corporations, and organizations. Our dedicated MICE organizing team is always on standby to handle any inquiries.

Location Hunting & Line Production

We’ve covered you if you’re a filmmaker searching for the perfect location. Thanks to our partnership with Cine Makers Pvt Ltd, a leading South Asian line production company, we can provide expert assistance and logistics for your project.

Flights

As a full-fledged travel agency, we can help you book flights and handle all your travel-related needs. Just let us know where you want to go, and we’ll find the best possible flights to get you there.

Hotel Bookings

Besides our packages and trips, we also offer independent hotel bookings for those who prefer to mix and match their travel arrangements. No matter what you need, we’re here to help you plan a seamless and stress-free trip.
