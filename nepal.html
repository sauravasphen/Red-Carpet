Red Carpet Journey welcomes you to the furthest corners of the Himalayas. Along on epic journeys, to see amazing places and meet equally amazing people. We take you to places, both the popular and unexplored. With us, you travel in style and comfort, have unique experiences and cultural immersion, while giving back to the communities you visit.
