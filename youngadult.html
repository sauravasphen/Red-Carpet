Young Adult Trips(18-29 Years)
Red Carpet’s Young Adult trips are custom designed for 18-29 year olds. We offer low-cost, action-packed and engaging sights, activities and experiences on these trips. Young Adult trips are an occasion for YA’s like you to socialize and create friendships that last. YA trips are greatly focused on social interactions with local communities, and creating an impact, sometimes through volunteering works, in the society.
Furthermore, these trips are stripped of all bells and whistles to the bare minimum needed to have a good time, in an effort to keep costs down.
Package meals? Nope. Good hotel? Lol you bet :) Private transport and internal flights? Ha Ha Ha
But it is the sense of adventure, society and camaraderie that counts, really.
